The Problem We Solve

Students often waste valuable time walking between buildings trying to find open study spaces, dining areas, or facilities. While existing apps show road traffic, they fail to provide insight into indoor congestion.

RushCheck bridges this gap by using AI-powered video analysis to estimate real-time congestion levels inside campus locations ‚Äî empowering students to make informed decisions before they arrive.
